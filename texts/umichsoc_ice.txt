The consumer sentiment index combines views on current and future economic conditions. In February 2024, the index tracking views on current economic conditions was 79.4, compared to 81.9 in January 2024, and 110.8 in 2019.In February 2024, the index tracking consumer expectations for future economic conditions was 75.2, compared to 77.1 in January 2024, and 86.5 in 2019.
